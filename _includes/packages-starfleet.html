Starfleet Academy: 4 years (13/-15)

This is a baseline package for all Starfleet characters, regardless of branch. Once a character graduates, they're a Cadet. From here, they're sent to one or more Cadet Cruises for six months each. These cruises help a cadet choose a branch school. It's common to go on two Cadet Cruises, but three or more mean that there's a problem.

Department Head School: 6 months (2/0)

This school is a pre-requisite for Command School. Characters who go through this school get an automatic one-rank promotion, (Typically from Ensign to Lt.J.G. as shown) and qualify them to lead a department on a starbase or a ship. Department heads are in control of their departments, but are responsible for the people under their command. (Sort of like an ad/disad of +5/-5.) Attending this school does not guarantee that a character heads a department. It merely qualifies the character for the job.

Medical Branch School: 3.5 years (15/10)

Attending this school makes the character a medical doctor, but it's far more advanced than what is needed. Doctor characters just need Paramedics, SS: Medical Science and a license to practice, with the optional addition of Surgery.

  • Surgery is both a science skill and a professional skill. The science skill would allow the character to know what surgeries are available, what sort of research has been done, and whether it's worth the risk to try it. The professional skill allows the character to perform the surgery.
  • A familiarity with PS: Surgeon allows a character to do most common surgeries without any trouble. Removing an appendix is a good example of a common surgery. Removing a bullet might require a familiarity if the wound was in the leg, but a full skill if it's near the heart.
  • PS: Surgeon should go hand-in-hand with Paramedics. A surgeon must know how to stabilize a patient.

Intelligence Branch Schools

Intelligence Branch School is split into three programs which have a different focus from standard schools.

  • Intelligence officers may attend Department Head School if they pursue a desk job, but they rarely attend Command School.
  • Sometimes officers are transferred to Intelligence after their time at the Academy. These officers attend one of the regular branch schools instead of this one.

Field Operations Branch: 2 Years (13/0)

This branch is what most people envision when they think of Starfleet Intelligence. They are trained to obtain information through social situations, investigation or even by theft.
